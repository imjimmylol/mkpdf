4. Numerical analysis of the consumption-saving problem

In this section, we solve a one-agent consumption-saving problem with an occasionally binding borrowing constraint. We show the deep learning method for three different objective function: the lifetime reward, and the Eulerand Bellmanequation residuals. Our experiments are designed to illustrate the role of hyperparameters in the solution, as well as to emphasize some useful features of the proposed method, in particular, its ability to accurately approximate kinks, its capacity to handle ill-conditioned problems and its scalability.
