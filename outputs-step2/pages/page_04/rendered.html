Our goal is to find a vector of parameters θ ∈ /Theta1 under which the parametric decision rule ϕ ( ·; θ) provides an accurate approximation of the optimal decision rule ϕ on a relevant domain. We do not assume a smoothness of the approximation function ϕ ( ·; θ) nor its linearity with respect to coefficients θ and state ( m t , s t ) . But we do require the problem to be time consistent, so that its solving amounts to finding time-invariant decision rules.
