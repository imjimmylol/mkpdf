The method based on the AiO expectation operator is our main method. However, we also develop and test various hybrid methods that construct two expectations separately. For example, we use a Monte Carlo method for constructing E ( m , s ) [ · ] , and we use some other methods for constructing E ϵ [ · ] such as quadrature and monomial rules, sparse grids, lowdiscrepancy sequences - we show these sensitivity results in Section 5 . Overall, we find that such hybrid methods are useful for small problems in which the construction of E ϵ [ · ] is inexpensive but the AiO expectation operator is critical for large problems, such as Krusell and Smith (1998) model studied in Section 6 .
